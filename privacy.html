SUI Price Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SUI Price Bot ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot service. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Quick Summary: SUI Price Bot is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not store personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: When you use our bot commands, we may temporarily log command usage for operational purposes
  • Discord User IDs: Only for the duration of command execution to respond to your requests
  • Server Information: Guild IDs where the bot operates (for functionality purposes only)

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal messages or chat history
  • Personal identifiable information (PII)
  • Voice data or media files
  • User activity outside of bot interactions
  • Financial information or trading data

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide SUI cryptocurrency price and market data
  • Respond to your bot commands
  • Maintain and improve bot functionality
  • Monitor for abuse and ensure service stability

4. Data Storage and Retention

Important: We do not permanently store user data. All interactions are processed in real-time and not retained.
  • No persistent user databases are maintained
  • Temporary logs may be kept for up to 7 days for operational purposes
  • Price data is cached temporarily (60 seconds) for performance optimization

5. Third-Party Services

Our bot uses the following third-party services:

  • CoinGecko API: For cryptocurrency price data (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Discord API: For bot functionality (subject to Discord's privacy policy)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there's minimal personal information to manage. However, you have the right to:

  • Stop using the bot at any time
  • Remove the bot from your Discord server
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use our bot.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.
